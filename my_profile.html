Mike Mcdonald, September 11 of 1989.


Is a Canadian poker player. McDonald is the youngest person to win an event on the European Poker Tour and the youngest person to win an event on the Epic Poker League (EPL).


On July 11, 2014, McDonald is ranked #7 on the Global poker index.


At 18 years of age, McDonald won the EPT German Open in Dortmund, Germany in February 2008 winning €933,600 ($1,370,161).
